# RoleReady AI — Demo Video Script
### Target length: 2 minutes 30 seconds

---

**[0:00–0:15] — THE HOOK (voiceover, no screen)**

"Meet Arjun. He just joined a company as a senior backend engineer with 8 years of experience.
On day one, the company hands him the same 40-hour onboarding course they give every new hire.
Arjun spends 32 of those 40 hours on things he already knows.
RoleReady AI fixes that."

---

**[0:15–0:35] — SHOW THE UPLOAD PAGE**

- Open http://localhost:3000/analyze
- Say: "This is RoleReady AI. You upload a resume and paste a job description."
- Click the Senior Engineer demo chip
- Say: "We've pre-loaded three demo profiles so you can see the system in action instantly."
- Hit Submit

---

**[0:35–0:55] — SHOW THE ANALYZING SCREEN**

- Watch the 6 pipeline steps tick green
- Say: "The system is now parsing the resume with LLaMA 3.1, running semantic matching with Sentence-BERT, sorting prerequisites with a graph algorithm, and matching against our fixed course catalog."
- Say: "Every step is real — no mocked data."

---

**[0:55–1:25] — SHOW THE RESULTS DASHBOARD**

- Land on /results
- Say: "Arjun's readiness score is 72%. He only needs 4 modules — not 40 hours."
- Point to the gap section: "The system identified 5 missing skills and 2 partial skills."
- Click WHY THIS MODULE on the first module
- Say: "This is our reasoning trace — every module explains exactly why it's here. Not in a log file. Right here in the UI."
- Click Mark Complete on one module
- Say: "Watch the readiness score update live."

---

**[1:25–1:55] — SHOW THE DEMO COMPARISON PAGE**

- Navigate to http://localhost:3000/demo
- Say: "Now watch what happens when we switch to the Fresh Graduate profile."
- Point to the three cards side by side
- Say: "Senior engineer — 72% readiness, 4 modules. Fresh graduate — 22% readiness, 7 modules. Operations worker — completely different skill domain, 45% readiness."
- Say: "Same system. Three completely different learning paths. That's adaptive onboarding."

---

**[1:55–2:15] — SHOW THE CATALOG PAGE**

- Navigate to http://localhost:3000/catalog
- Say: "One more thing — zero hallucinations."
- Type 'Docker' in the search bar
- Say: "The AI can only pick from these 30 real courses. It never invents a course name. Every recommendation is grounded in this fixed catalog."

---

**[2:15–2:30] — CLOSE**

- Go back to the results page
- Say: "RoleReady AI — built for IISc Bangalore's Adaptive Onboarding Hackathon."
- Say: "Resume in. Personalized roadmap out. Only what you're missing. Nothing you already know."
- Freeze on the dashboard

---

### Recording tips:
- Record at 1440p or 1080p, no lower
- Use QuickTime screen recording on Mac or OBS on Windows
- Speak slowly — 130 words per minute max
- Do one full rehearsal before recording
- Upload to YouTube unlisted or Google Drive and paste link in submission
